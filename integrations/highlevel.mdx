## Integration Steps

You can setup your highlevel integration in 5 simple steps.

1. Create a new or use an existing device in the Chatlevel app
1. Connect your whatsapp account to the device
2. Add your highlevel subaccount from within the integrations tab in Chatlevel
3. Bind your subaccount to the device in the Chatlevel devices table
5. Activate our 'Whatsapp gateway' conversation provider in your highlevel subaccount
